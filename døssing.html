Døssing

Process:

Døssing is a Danish jazz ensemble of siblings from Aarhus. Their sound is electro-jazzed and synth-driven instrumental music that bridges different jazz traditions, involving dubbed grooves and catchy melodies. As their album ‘All Inclusive’ is shaped around arriving at a hotel we set out to explore this vivid universe and visualize it with the music.

In our efforts to describe what went on in Hotel All Inclusive, its inhabitants, its sensory landscapes and sounds we had a series of talks with the ensemble, exchanging ideas and developing a number of concepts to set the artist's direction. The result was a series of creative productions manifesting ideas and setting the visual aesthetic of the ensemble.

Products:

Our productions with Døssing included: concept development and copywriting in relation to a short film anthology, Promotional materials in a series of short scenes, press material in the form of a series of photoshoots as well as a live session and coverarts for the singles ‘all inclusive’ & ‘chai wallah Shub’.
