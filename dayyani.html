Dayyani

Process:

In this shoot, we wanted to signify a new chapter of DAYYANIs career in the music industry. Moving from an independent upcoming artist to signing with a record label and being picked up by some of Denmark's most prominent music magazines and radio stations. This shoot showcases a newfound seriousness and fierceness in DAYYANI personally and as a musical artist.



Products:

A press photoshoot of musical artist DAYYANI, for her press campaign and tour poster in 2022.
